# 🌎🌍🌏Hi,
The idea is to release a text into the wild, freely available for free.
You can read it, of course.
But the most important thing is that it's participatory.
If you want to change a sentence, a chapter, or even the ending, go ahead, have fun!

Total freedom with the text.

Suggest, make changes, correct things, or even take the text with you to create your own version.

Come and get your hands dirty and modify it.

It's totally the open source spirit applied to a book.

And [the license, the WTFPL](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/WTFPL), it's clear: it allows you to do absolutely anything, even to resell it if you want.

Fancy a look? Come see, and if it excites you... well, have fun with wink-wink!

So it's on GitHub (it's the "CANON" version):<br>

- <https://github.com/Dfalm-Original/jiuuijh><br>If you don't know it, or you don't know how it works, or you're scared, don't panic;
- There's also a repository (a "[J I T](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juste-%C3%A0-temps_\(gestion\))") on Quora:<br> https://paoriiujiuuijh.quora.com/
- And if you don't know Quora, or it scares you, or you "*just*" want to read; <br>
- It's possible with a good old-fashioned website, this is the "[rolling release](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rolling_release)" version:
